Digital Photography Project (Ages 3 to 8)

Project 1: Shape Hunting

Activity 1 -- Brainstorm

  1. List or draw all the shapes you know.
  2. Can you identify the shapes in these ordinary items?
©Copyright George Washington. All rights reserved.






Activity 2 -- Shoot

Grab your digital camera and photograph the shapes you see in ordinary things.

Activity 3 -- Upload

Upload 3 of your best photos to the "Shape Hunting" album on Google Photos.

Activity 4 -- Gallery Talk

Let's get together on Zoom and chat about our photos! What did you find? Where did you go?

Happy Hunting!
